Consignment Business Process / Second-hand Shops

Product Consignment in Odoo

This module allows you to have processes for Consignment business and Second-hand shops.

Consignment is a business arrangement in which a business, also referred to as a consignee, agrees to pay a seller, or consignor, for merchandise after the item sells.

Consignment businesses are typically retail stores that specialize in a particular type of consumer product.

Main Features:

  • Allow you to create and manage a Consignment Item(s) / Product(s) in the system.
  • History of Consignment Purchase and Sales on Product form.
  • Allow your purchase team to create Consignment RFQ / Purchase orders and allow them to validate/confirm it.
  • Allow the purchase team to print Consignment Agreement from purchase order.
  • Allow the Sales team to create a Consignment Quote / Sales order.
  • Allow Inventory team to process / transfer Consignment Incoming Shipment.
  • Allow your Inventory team to process / transfer Consignment Delivery Order.
  • Allow users to print Consignment report in PDF.
  • Portal for consignor. Allow them to view consignment items and consignments on portal my account.
  • The Point of Sales order will show the purchase order line when POS order gets confirmed.
