Data Collection and Transmission

Data Collected

To provide seamless invoice financing through the AR Funding integration, we collect and process certain data directly from the Odoo platform. The data collected includes:

  • Invoice Data Details of the invoices selected for financing, including invoice number, date, due date, amounts, and customer information.
  • Customer Information Business name, contact details, and relevant financial history to assess eligibility for financing.
  • Account Information Business account details necessary for transferring funds, including bank account numbers, routing information, and other payment-related details.
  • Transaction Data Information related to the financing transactions, including amounts advanced, fees charged, and repayment terms.
Data Transmission
  • Secure Transmission All data transmitted between Odoo and AR Funding servers is encrypted using industry-standard security protocols (SSL/TLS) to ensure confidentiality and integrity.
  • Data Usage The collected data is used strictly for the purposes of evaluating financing applications, processing funding transactions, and managing ongoing financing arrangements.
  • Data Retention We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
Privacy and Security
  • confidentiality We adhere to strict data privacy standards and do not share your data with third parties, except as necessary to provide the financing services or comply with legal requirements.
  • User Control You maintain control over the data shared through the app and can request the deletion or modification of your data at any time by contacting our support team.
